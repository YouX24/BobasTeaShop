About us

Welcome to Boba's Tea Place! Here at Boba's Tea Place we offer the very best in Boba Tea. Boba's Tea Place was founded with the idea of being able to give people an experience of trying out some delicious drinks, the best Boba Tea possible. At Boba's Tea Place we are dedicated to providing customers with the finest boba tea that they deserve!
